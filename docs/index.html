UVDetective

Taking a step towards monitoring Vitamin D deficiency.

Every hour, one person dies due to the side effects of Vitamin D Deficiency. In the US alone, about half of Americans suffer from vitamin D deficiency.

As students spend more time inside, UVDetective combats the increasing risks of vitamin D deficiency by accurately monitoring the child's vitamin D levels.
